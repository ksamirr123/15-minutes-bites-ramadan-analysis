# 🍽️ 15 Minutes Bites — Ramadan Food Delivery Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed 800+ delivery orders for a Ramadan food delivery platform 
to identify top revenue drivers and peak demand patterns.

**Tools:** MySQL · SQL · Power BI  
**Year:** 2024

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

| Finding | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Top revenue drivers | 3 platters drove 70%+ of total revenue |
| Peak demand window | 30-minute spike before iftar every day |
| Total orders analyzed | 800+ |
| Menu items analyzed | 15 items |

---

## 💡 Business Impact
- Kitchen prep restructured around top 3 high-performing platters
- Delivery riders pre-positioned during the 30-minute iftar window
- Reduced waste by deprioritizing low-performing menu items
